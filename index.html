style and inspiration

Nostalgia has always been such a dominant force in my life that has shaped who I am. A lot of my hobbies and interests today are nostalgic things, like old media and fashion that were popular when I was growing up. That girly, bright, and colorful 2000s style prominent in games and shows is a major inspiration for me today! Icons like Barbie and Polly Pocket raised me, and I try to encapsulate a similar theme of unabashed youthful femininity through my art!

I'm also heavily inspired by dramatic and maximalist styles like Gyaru (specifically Himekaji and Agejo), McBling, Frutiger Aero, and Pink Paris Design. Lots of online and old web aesthetics that I loved growing up and continue to surround myself in!

some of my artistic inspirations are:

  • Jeffrey Fulvimari - signature sketchy linework style of chic characters
  • Park Suran - enakei drawings and 2000s flash games style
  • Takashi Murakami - vibrant and coloful iconic style
  • Karen Duncan & Samantha Stringle (Bang on the Door) - 2000s tween character groovy chick
  • Andrea Kett - detailed and whimsical feminine designs
  • Polly Pocket (2000s) - 2000s flat webisode and flash games style
  • Boutiqiabe - posh french cats in boudoir and elegant settings
